US States Wishlist

The states I haven't visited yet ranked in order of how much I want to see them

July 29, 2019

Since I started working as a software developer, I have been fortunate to travel extensively throughout the United States for conferences and professional development, but also to see friends who are spread across the country. As many do, one day I hope to visit all 50. With 24 states left, I have ranked the remaining states below ordered by how much excitement I have to explore each place.

  21. North Dakota
  22. Nebraska

  24. West Virginia
